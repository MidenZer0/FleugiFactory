Have you ever dreamed earning money by slaping people ? Or ever felt the primal urge to let your fists do the talking ? What if I told you that your dream could be true ?

At Fleugi Factory, we're all about the raw, unfiltered power of slap-boxing. We train warriors ready to dominate the Slap Fighting Championship no matter who you are. Why join us? Because we're not just a club—we're a brotherhood of adrenaline junkies and fighters who thrive on competition.

Our coaches are battle-hardened veterans who know what it takes to win, and our facilities are designed to push you to your limits and beyond. If you're ready to step into the ring, feel the rush of combat, and leave your mark on the world of slap-boxing, Fleugi Factory is your battleground. Come join the ranks of the fearless and let's make some serious noise together!
